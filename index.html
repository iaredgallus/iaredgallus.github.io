About Me

Hey there! My name is Jared Hawn. I'm a western Massachusetts native living in Chicago, where I work remotely as a system analyst centered on data and web design. My internet alias, iaredgallus, is my first and last name transposed into Latin.

My everyday interests include hiking, writing, ultimate frisbee, space, world building, language creation, urban design, and behavioral psychology.
