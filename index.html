About Me

Welcome to my site! I'm a Massachusetts native living in Chicago, where I work remotely as a system analyst centered on data and web design. My internet alias, iaredgallus, is my first and last name transposed to Latin.

I have an academic background in theology (B.S.), linguistics (M.A.), and computer science. On this site, you'll find my resume, a programming portfolio, and links to some of my creative projects.

My interests include running, hiking, space, ultimate frisbee, urban design, behavioral psychology, fiction-world building, and language creation. You'll undoubtedly see evidence of these interests as you explore the site!
